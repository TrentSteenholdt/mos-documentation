    Home Dashboard

    Overview

    The Home page provides a personalized dashboard showing your welcome message, recent news, quick actions, summary charts, and recent activity. Content adapts based on your role and selected association.

    How to Access: Click Home

    Page Sections

    Welcome Message

    • Greets you by name
    • Shows important system notices or license information
    • Displays current system version number

    News Section

    • Shows the most recent news articles from your association
    • Displays up to 3 recent items
    • Click any item to read the full article

    Quick Actions

    Shortcuts to common tasks:

    For All Users:

    • New diary entry: Create a new assessment diary entry
    • New plan: Create a new training or development plan

    For Administrators:

    • Additional shortcuts for review and management tasks
    • Access depends on your role and selected group

    Summary Charts

    Visual summaries showing:

    • Your assessment and plan statistics
    • Login activity
    • Optional filter to focus on current season or year

    Note

    Charts update based on the time period you select

    Recent Activity

    • Timeline of your recent actions
    • Shows diary entries, plans, exams, and reviews
    • Click any item to view details

    Common Tasks

    How to Create a New Diary Entry

    1. On the Home page, find Quick Actions
    2. Click New diary entry
    3. Result: The diary entry form opens

    How to Create a New Plan

    1. On the Home page, find Quick Actions
    2. Click New plan
    3. Result: The plan form opens

    How to Read a News Article

    1. On the Home page, find the News section
    2. Click any article title
    3. Result: The full article opens with any attachments

    How to Filter Charts by Season

    1. Find the Summary charts section
    2. Look for the season or year filter option
    3. Select your preferred time period
    4. Result: Charts update to show data for the selected period

    Note: This option appears when available for your association

    How to View Activity Details

    1. Find the Recent activity section
    2. Click any activity item
    3. Result: The full details open

    Troubleshooting

    Administrator shortcuts do not appear

    Symptoms: You do not see admin links in Quick Actions

    How to Fix:

    1. Check which group you have selected
    2. Verify your role includes administrator access
    3. Contact your association administrator to confirm your permissions

    Common Causes:

    • You do not have administrator access for the selected group
    • Your role does not include admin features

    Charts are empty

    Symptoms: Summary charts show no data

    How to Fix:

    1. Try selecting a different time period in the filter
    2. Check back later after completing some activities
    3. Verify you have diary entries or plans recorded

    Common Causes:

    • No activity recorded for the selected time period
    • Filters are too narrow
    • You have just joined and have no history yet

    News section is empty

    Symptoms: No news articles appear

    How to Fix:

    1. Navigate to the full News page to see all articles
    2. Ask your administrator if news has been published
    3. Verify your membership status

    Common Causes:

    • No recent news published
    • News was published to other subgroups
