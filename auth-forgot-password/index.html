    Forgot Password: Recover Your Account

    Access Level

    Public (before authentication)

    Overview

    Password recovery is a three-step process that sends a reset link to your registered email after you confirm your identity with your secret question.

    How to Access: On the sign in page, click Forgot my password

    Step 1: Identify Account

    1. Enter your username or email address.
    2. Click Next.

    Validation:

    • Username/email must exist in MOS.
    • If multiple accounts share an email, you may be asked to use your username.

    Errors and fixes:

    • "Account not found": Check for typos; try your other email; contact your association.
    • "Too many attempts": Wait a few minutes and try again.

    Step 2: Secret Question

    1. Your saved secret question is displayed.
    2. Enter the secret answer (case-sensitive).
    3. Click Submit.

    Tips:

    • Answers are case-sensitive; match your original capitalisation.
    • Remove extra spaces; avoid copy-paste quirks.

    Errors and fixes:

    • "Incorrect answer": Try your alternate phrasing; consider case/capitalisation.
    • "No secret question set": Contact your association to reset security settings.

    Step 3: Reset Email

    • A password reset email is sent to your registered email address.
    • The email contains a time-limited link to create a new password.

    If the email doesn’t arrive:

    • Check Spam/Junk.
    • Search for the subject like "Reset your Match Official System password".
    • Ask your IT to allowlist the sender domain.

    After Reset

    1. Click the link in the email.
    2. Enter a new password and confirm it.
    3. Submit to complete the reset.

    Password rules:

    • Minimum 7 characters.
    • Mix of uppercase, lowercase, and numbers.
    • Symbols recommended.
    • Avoid common words and reuse from other sites.

    Troubleshooting

    • No email received: Verify the correct email is on your account via an admin; re-run the flow; check Spam/Junk.
    • Link expired: Re-run the Forgot Password flow to receive a fresh link.
    • Locked out: Too many attempts may temporarily lock recovery; wait and retry.
    • No access to email: Contact your association to update your email.

    Tips

    • ✅ Set a memorable secret answer and store it securely.
    • ✅ Use a password manager to generate and store strong passwords.
    • ✅ Change your password regularly and avoid reuse.
